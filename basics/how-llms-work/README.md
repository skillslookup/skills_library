# 🧠 How LLMs Work

## What is this?
Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI systems trained on massive amounts of text. They are designed to understand, summarize, and generate human-like language.

## Why it matters
LLMs are the engine behind tools like ChatGPT. Understanding how they work helps you use them more effectively.

## How it works (simple)
Think of an LLM like a **Super-Powered Autocomplete**. 
When you type "The cat sat on the...", the AI isn't "thinking"; it's calculating that the word "mat" has a 99% chance of being high-quality based on billions of sentences it has seen before.
