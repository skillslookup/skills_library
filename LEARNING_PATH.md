# 🗺️ AI Skills Learning Path

Welcome to your structured journey! This roadmap is designed to take you from **curiosity** to **competence**. Follow the steps below for the best experience.

---

## 🟢 Step 1: The Foundations (Basics)
Learn the core concepts without writing a single line of code.

1.  **[What is AI?](./basics/what-is-ai)**: Understand the "magic" behind the screen.
2.  **[How LLMs Work](./basics/how-llms-work)**: Discover why AI is a "next-word predictor."
3.  **[Prompt Engineering](./basics/prompt-engineering)**: Master the art of communicating with AI.

---

## 🟡 Step 2: Adding Memory (Intermediate)
Once you can talk to AI, learn how to give it new information.

1.  **[Embeddings](./intermediate/embeddings)**: How machines "feel" the meaning of words.
2.  **[Vector Databases](./intermediate/vector-databases)**: Storing the "meaning" for later use.
3.  **[RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)](./intermediate/rag)**: Teaching AI about your own private data.

---

## 🔴 Step 3: Autonomy and Advanced Tools (Advanced)
Move from static responses to dynamic, acting agents.

1.  **[Agents](./advanced/agents)**: Give the AI tools (like Search or Calculator).
2.  **[Fine-Tuning](./advanced/fine-tuning)**: Training the AI to speak in your voice.
3.  **[Multi-Agent Systems](./advanced/multi-agent-systems)**: Building a "team" of AIs.

---

## 🚀 Step 4: Build Your First Project
Put your learning into action with these step-by-step guides.

1.  **[Build a Chatbot](./projects/build-a-chatbot)**: The classic starting point.
2.  **[RAG App](./projects/rag-app)**: Chat with your own PDFs.
3.  **[AI Agent](./projects/ai-agent)**: Build an AI that can book flights or research topics.

---

### 💡 Tips for Success
- **Experiment Often**: Run the code in the `/examples` folders.
- **Don't Rush**: AI is a fast-moving field, but the fundamentals (Embeddings, RAG) remain constant.
- **Join the Community**: Share what you build!
